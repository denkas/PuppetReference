Solution Provider Partners

Puppet Labs Solution Providers use their expertise and technical skills to resell Puppet Enterprise as part of a complete IT automation solution. Partnering with us gives you a robust service revenue opportunity that increases your profitability and helps differentiate you from the competition.

From training and education to marketing and sales support, our Solution Provider program is designed to give you the resources you need to deliver the right automation solutions to your customers.

Apply Now
Silver - Receive a base level of discount and deal registration bonus for approved opportunities. Minimum $100,000 annual revenue commitment and at least one Puppet Certified Professional required.

Gold - Receive an increased discount over our Silver level and deal registration bonus for approved opportunities. Minimum $200,000 annual revenue commitment and at least two Puppet Certified Professionals required.

Download the Solution Provider Program Overview

Service Delivery Partners

Service Delivery Partners use their IT consulting experience and expertise to help customers implement Puppet Enterprise and deliver services on behalf of Puppet Labs. All prospective Service Delivery Partners are carefully evaluated. If accepted into the program, consultants must complete a rigorous training and assessment process to become Puppet Certified Consultants.

By investing in the enablement and certification of your consultants, authorized Service Delivery Partners will benefit from market differentiation, increased credibility with our customers, and additional service revenue streams.

Apply Now
Silver - Must have an executed subcontracting agreement in place with Puppet Labs and a minimum of two Puppet Certified Consultants on staff.

Gold - Must have a minimum of four Puppet Certified Consultants on staff and a proven record of successful Puppet Labs engagements. Gold Service Delivery Partners are eligible for customer referrals in addition to subcontracting on behalf of Puppet Labs.

Download the Service Delivery Program Overview

Authorized Training Partners

Puppet Labs Authorized Training Partners provide Puppet users with access to quality training throughout the world. Authorized Training Partners are carefully selected to deliver Puppet Labs training courses in their designated regions and use Puppet Labs developed courseware to educate customers on Puppet best practices. Benefits include instructor education and certification, instructor and student course materials, and marketing of classes on Puppet Labs’ website.
